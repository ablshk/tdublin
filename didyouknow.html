Some fun facts about Ireland:

  1. Ireland is one of the few countries that don't have snakes.
  2. The Titanic's last port of call was Cobh, County Cork.
  3. The Wild Atlantic Way in the west of Ireland is the longest coastal driving route in the world
  4. Ireland is home to the world's oldest Yatch Club, the Royal Cork Yatch Club.
  5. The place with the longest name in Ireland is Muckanaghederdauhaulia, which translates to "pig shaped hill between two seas."
  6. Ireland is the second highest consumer of tea in the world.
  7. Newgrange passage tomb is a prehistoric monument that predates the Stonehenge and the Pyramids!
